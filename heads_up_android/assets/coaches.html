Coaches

  • Coaches are typically volunteer parents or family members helping the players learn and enjoy the game. Parents are encouraged to support the coach at all time.
  • Coaches are allowed on the field to direct players according to need and division. Coaches must move to the sidelines before the snap of the ball.
  • Coaches are expected to adhere to NFL FLAG powered by USA Football philosophies, coaching guidelines and codes of conduct.
  • Only two coaches per team are allowed on the sidelines.
